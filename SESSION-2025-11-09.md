## üí° Key Insights & Learnings

### Product Strategy
1. **Start with detailers, not mechanics** - Simpler service catalog, faster to PMF
2. **Lead with routing + deposits** - Core differentiators vs. Calendly/Square
3. **Sell-first approach** - Validate MVP (20 paid pilots) before full V1 build
4. **Payment-first revenue model** - Platform fee (1.0-1.5%) on payment volume doubles ARPU

### Technical Strategy
1. **Multi-tenant by design** - All data isolated by `tenant_id` with RLS
2. **API-first architecture** - Powers web, mobile, third-party integrations
3. **Stripe handles PCI compliance** - Platform never stores raw card data
4. **Horizontal scaling via Azure Container Apps** - Auto-scale 1-10 instances

### Go-to-Market Strategy
1. **Creator channels** - Partner with YouTube detailers (rev-share)
2. **Supplier partnerships** - Bundle trials with chemical distributors
3. **SEO utilities** - Price calculator, scheduler templates (lead generation)
4. **Referral loop** - Incentivize customers to share branded booking pages
